FSR Monitoring

Season-by-Season Efficiency Drift

FSR enforcement froze wages in Ligue 1 from 2023 onwards. Track how mean efficiency and mispricing rates evolved across all three seasons covered by the thesis data.

Note on responsible use. Efficiency scores model publicly available performance and wage data. They do not capture contract bonuses, image rights, agent fees, injury depth, or tactical role nuance. A low efficiency score is a hypothesis worth investigating — not a verdict. This tool complements scouting, medical review, and private compensation intelligence; it does not replace them.
